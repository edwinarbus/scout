Adoptable Dogs

Adoptable dogs needing urgent help  |  How to adopt a dog from OAS  |  Foster dogs attending adoption events

To adopt, come on by during our open adoption hours which are Thursdays 12 to 6:30 pm, and Fridays/Saturdays/Sundays from 12 to 3pm, no appointment needed. Dogs listed as “I am in a foster home” are generally not at the shelter and will require emailing in your adoption questionnaire. View the adoption events foster dogs will be attending

Are you a veteran? We’ve partnered with Pets for Patriots! Adopt a dog from OAS and get some perks through this unique program, including waived adoption fees.

Ages:  baby = less than 12 months   |   young = 1-4 years  |  adult = 4-7 years  | senior = 7+ years

Today, Wednesday, July 1st, we have 68 big dogs in the shelter. Our shelter capacity is 73 kennels, and we should always have several kennels open for incoming dogs, so we are currently AT CAPACITY.
